Frequently Asked Questions

What is FlashLab?

FlashLab is a free web app that connects to your Kilter Board via Bluetooth. You can browse thousands of climbs, control LEDs, generate playlists, log your sends, and share sessions with friends — all from your phone.

Which Kilter Boards does FlashLab support?

All sizes: Original boards (7×10, 8×12, 12×12, 12×14, 16×12) and Homewall kits (7×10, 8×12, 10×10, 10×12) with both Full Ride and Mainline LED layouts.

Does FlashLab work on iPhone or iPad?

iOS/Safari does not support Web Bluetooth, so you can't connect directly. Use Party Mode instead — have an Android or desktop Chrome user host, then join from your iPhone with a PIN code.

Do I need an account?

No. Browse climbs, connect to your board, and generate playlists without signing up. Create a free account with just your email to unlock the logbook, community ratings, and tags.

Does it work without internet?

Yes. FlashLab is a Progressive Web App you can install on your home screen. The climb database is cached locally and Bluetooth control works entirely offline.

What is Party Mode?

One device hosts the Bluetooth connection while others join via PIN or QR code. Everyone can browse climbs and send routes to the board — no more passing one phone around.
